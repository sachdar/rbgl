Licensed Custom Clearance in Pakistan

RBGL is officially licensed to provide custom clearance services in Pakistan. From Karachi Ports (KICT, QICT, PICT,KGTL, SAPT) to dry ports in Lahore and Islamabad, we ensure smooth clearance of imports and exports. Our team specializes in HS code classification, duty/tax calculation, and compliance with Pakistan Customs regulations.

Case Study: Import Clearance — Karachi Ports (KICT, QICT, PICT,KGTL, SAPT) & — Gwadar Port

Background: A machinery importer required rapid clearance for a time‑sensitive shipment arriving at Karachi Port.

Challenges: Complex HS classification, duty/tax optimization, and coordination with multiple stakeholders under tight deadlines.

Execution:

  • Pre‑arrival document review and HS code validation
  • Duty/tax calculation with applicable exemptions
  • Filing GD, payment processing, and examination scheduling
  • Coordination with terminal, customs, and transport for swift gate‑out

Outcome: Cleared within the planned window, minimizing storage and demurrage—full compliance and transparent documentation.

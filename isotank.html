ISO Tank Services

Secure, compliant transport for liquid bulk cargo across China–Iran and global corridors.

Why ISO Tanks?

ISO tanks are the backbone of safe liquid bulk logistics. Built to international standards, they allow chemicals, food‑grade liquids, and hazardous cargo to move seamlessly across borders. At RBGL, we deploy modern ISO tanks with full certification, ensuring compliance with IMDG, ADR, and local regulations.

Key Advantages

  • Secure containment for hazardous and non‑hazardous liquids
  • Food‑grade tanks for edible oils, juices, and beverages
  • Certified compliance with international safety codes
  • Reduced risk of leakage, contamination, and spillage
  • Efficient multimodal transport: road, rail, and sea

Our Capabilities

  • Fleet of modern ISO tanks with valid CSC plates
  • Specialized handling for chemicals, petrochemicals, and food liquids
  • Route planning across China–Iran corridors
  • Customs clearance and documentation support
  • Real‑time tracking and monitoring

Case Study: Methanol Transport — Ningbo to Bandar Abbas

Background: A petrochemical client required safe transport of methanol from Ningbo, China to Bandar Abbas, Iran.

Challenges: Handling hazardous liquid cargo, ensuring compliance with IMDG codes, coordinating multimodal transport, and securing permits across two jurisdictions.

Execution:

  • ISO tanks certified for hazardous liquids were deployed.
  • Road transport from Ningbo plant to port, with strict safety protocols.
  • Sea freight to Bandar Abbas with continuous monitoring.
  • Customs clearance and onward distribution to inland Iranian facilities.

Outcome: Delivered safely and on schedule, with zero leakage or compliance issues. Demonstrated RBGL’s ability to manage hazardous liquid logistics across China–Iran corridors.

Cargo Consolidation & Transshipment

Global groupage solutions — connecting shippers, optimizing costs, and enabling seamless transshipment worldwide.

Global Consolidation Expertise

RBGL provides cargo consolidation and transshipment services across major trade corridors. By combining multiple consignments into shared containers, we reduce costs, optimize space, and streamline customs processes. Our transshipment hubs in Asia, the Middle East, and Europe ensure smooth cargo flow between origins and destinations.

Key Benefits

  • Lower freight costs through shared container space
  • Efficient transshipment at global hub ports
  • Optimized container utilization and reduced carbon footprint
  • Reliable schedules and faster turnaround
  • Compliance with international shipping standards

Our Capabilities

  • LCL (Less‑than‑Container Load) consolidation
  • Groupage services for multiple shippers
  • Transshipment hubs in Dubai, Singapore, Rotterdam
  • Custom clearance support for consolidated cargo
  • End‑to‑end tracking and documentation

Case Study: Electronics Consolidation & Transshipment

Background: Electronics manufacturers in East Asia needed cost‑efficient shipping to the Middle East, Persian Gulf, Red sea sector and African Markets, but each had partial loads unsuitable for full container booking.

Challenges: Coordinating multiple suppliers, ensuring smooth transshipment at hub ports, and maintaining visibility for all stakeholders.

Execution:

  • Consolidated LCL shipments from different part of the world, into shared containers
  • Transshipped via Dubai,Malaysia, Djibouti and Jeddah hub for onward distribution to Persian Gulf, GCC markets and Red Sea sector market.
  • Optimized packing and documentation for multi‑origin cargo
  • Provided unified tracking and status updates to all shippers

Outcome: Reduced freight costs by 30%, ensured timely delivery to multiple destinations, and enhanced supply chain efficiency through RBGL’s transshipment network.

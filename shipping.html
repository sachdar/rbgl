TIR Carnet — Persian Gulf to CIS Countries

Seamless customs transit across Central Asia and beyond, powered by RBGL’s expertise.

What is TIR Carnet?

The TIR Carnet is an international customs transit system that allows goods to move across multiple borders with minimal checks, reducing delays and costs. RBGL leverages its TIR Carnet license to connect Iran with all CIS countries, ensuring compliant, secure, and efficient transport.

Coverage — All CIS Countries

RBGL provides TIR Carnet services from Iran to the following CIS member states:

  • Armenia
  • Azerbaijan
  • Afghanistan
  • Kazakhstan
  • Kyrgyzstan
  • Tajikistan
  • Turkmenistan
  • Uzbekistan

Key Advantages

  • Single customs document valid across multiple countries
  • Reduced border delays and inspections
  • Secure sealing of cargo under TIR regulations
  • Cost savings through streamlined transit
  • Trusted by customs authorities worldwide
